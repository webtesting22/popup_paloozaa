Our Story...

Popup Palooza is the brainchild of Astha Sanghvi & Aksha Shah, a dynamic duo. Astha, a fashion designer herself, possesses a keen eye for fashion and styling, while Aksha leads one of Ahmedabad's most esteemed media houses, boasting a vast network within society. Popup Palooza was born from our passion for introducing Ahmedabad to a diverse array of culinary and fashion experiences from across the nation, showcasing well-established brands cherished by many in other cities, whether it be a café from Mumbai or a clothing label from Delhi. Allow us to introduce Popup Palooza, a groundbreaking concept aimed at redefining Ahmedabad's literary and lifestyle scene. Our brand pledges an unmatched experience, seamlessly blending literature, art, and exclusive offerings, inviting you to transition from a passive observer to an active participant in a realm of creativity and refinement. Each of our trunk shows is meticulously curated to showcase unique and unconventional clothing and lifestyle brands, alongside beloved cafes, bridging the gap between aspiration and accessibility, and offering a glimpse into the cultural richness found in various cities across the country. This isn't merely a one-time event; it signifies the commencement of a series of specially curated trunk shows throughout the year, each one promising to surpass the last in captivation. With Popup Palooza, we're crafting a narrative that celebrates the essence of literature, style, and community, welcoming you to join us on a journey to elevate Ahmedabad's cultural landscape.
